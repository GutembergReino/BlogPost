My dev routine

Published on December 1, 2023

The adventures of working as a software developer

Introduction:

Hey folks! Today, I'm going to share a bit about what my life is like as a developer. No superhero capes, but there's definitely a lot of caffeine involved. Let's take a laid-back look into the world of code and algorithms!

6:00 - The Coffee Ritual:

My day kicks off with the sacred coffee ritual. It's not just a cup; it's an almost religious experience. Choosing the right bean is crucial, and the caffeine intake is measured in units of "Happy Devs."

7:00 - Morning Meeting:

We dive into the morning meeting, more like a comedy episode. Debates about naming variables and attempts to decipher emojis in the last commit are all part of the fun.

8:00 - The Saga of the Infamous Bug:

Ah, the mysterious bug that nobody understood. It's a true treasure hunt, where I become a detective, scouring the code for that elusive error probably caused by a rebellious semicolon.

13:00 - Lunchtime and the Revitalizing Power of Pizza:

Lunch break is sacred. And the official dev food? Pizza, of course. Who said it's not healthy when we're solving code problems?

15:00 - Battling Procrastination:

The battle against procrastination is real. Between funny memes, videos on "How to Train Your Algorithm," and debates about programming languages, the struggle to stay focused is constant.

18:00 - Code Review or Brazilian Reality Show?

Code review turns into a Brazilian reality show. Witty comments, improvement suggestions, and debates about indentation. It feels like we're watching an episode of "Dev's Got Talent," Brazilian style.

20:00 - Update Time:

Updating the operating system is like a mystical ritual. It feels like we're performing a ceremony to ensure all dependencies remain happy and functional.

22:00 - Epic Farewell:

I bid farewell to the workday with an epic phrase like "May the force of code be with us all." Sometimes followed by a quick visit to Stack Overflow to solve that last-minute problem.

Conclusion:

My routine as a developer is filled with comedic twists, intriguing challenges, and, of course, a generous amount of coffee. So, the next time you see me with a contemplative expression in front of the monitor, remember: behind every line of code, there's a hilarious story and a laid-back dev mastering the world of development. Until the next code adventure!
